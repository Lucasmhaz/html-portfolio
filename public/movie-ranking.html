The Best Movies According to Lucas

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Northman

An action packed viking revenge story.
"I will avenge you. I will honor our blood. I will cut the thread of fate."

The Outsiders

A film adaption of a classic YA novel by S.E. Hinton.
"Stay gold, Pony Boy, stay gold."

Django Unchained

Quentin Tarantino's American slave revenge film.
"I like the way you die, boy."
